Mission

Welcome to the Still Here website. This website was created by 6 students from Brooklyn South to archive our experience in the Names, Not Numbers® program of Holocaust education and rememberance and to serve as an archive of our 8 day trip to Poland, where we visited the cities of Warsaw, Lublin, and Krakow to learn about Jewish heritage in Poland, while witnessing the places that atrocities occured. Along with that, it is also a research and resource center that can be accessed by anybody curious about fighting hate (antisemetism or not) or want to learn more about Poland.

We hope that you will take something away from this website. If you instead want to give and contribute, you can send resources and information by emailing us. Check either the "Contact" menu or the "What can I do?" menu.

Take a look at the Names, Not Numbers® program and the schools and names involved with this archival project.
