Meet Adam.

On the Poland Trip, we met our Tour Guide, Adam Koren.

In today’s world, hate is rampant and is the driving force of many terrible events, and it's essential to recognize hate and try to stop it before it snowballs into something much worse.

This can be done through:

Education

Educate others about what you have learned and tell them to pursue knowledge on subjects themselves so that they can gain a clear idea of the circumstances behind something. Knowing about the hateful actions that lead up to genocides is a very important step in preventing them.

Communication

Communicating with others and making sure that their concerns are heard is the best way to prevent disputes from happening, helping to prevent the spread of hate.

Understanding

Lastly, gaining an understanding of the different sides of an issue and trying to find common ground is also a good way to prevent hate from spreading.

These things, in combination with one another, can prove very effective in combating hate in all aspects of our lives, which can help ensure that terrible events like the Holocaust don’t keep happening
